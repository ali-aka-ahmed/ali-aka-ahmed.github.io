hello world :)

or specifically sf.

i wanted to ship something as i returned. something beautiful.
something that i wish i had. as an ode to my past self.

so i built this journaling app for myself.

two years ago, i left sf.
one day i'll write about why.

this great city is filled with people building the future.
that's a lot of weight to carry.
i once carried that weight and was (nearly) crushed by it.
and so right here (see pic), i started to journal.

this quick product is a love letter to my past self.
to those carrying that weight. and those that will carry it.

i hope this makes the weight feel lighter.

how people feel about you is in large part driven by how you make them feel about themselves.

i want this product to make you feel good about yourself :) enjoy.

- ali
